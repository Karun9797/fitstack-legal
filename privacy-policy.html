Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Date — June 6, 2026] FitStack is developed by [Karun Sunuwar]. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user. ──────────────────────────────────────── 1. Information We Collect ──────────────────────────────────────── We do not collect or store any personal information directly. However, the app uses third-party services that may collect data automatically: a) Google AdMob FitStack displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following to serve relevant ads: - Device identifiers (Advertising ID) - IP address - General location (country/region level) - App usage and interaction data Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy b) Camera The camera is used solely to detect and count exercise repetitions in real time. Camera data is processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any server. c) Local Storage The app stores your workout session history and rep counts locally on your device using your device's internal storage. This data never leaves your device. ──────────────────────────────────────── 2. How We Use Your Information ──────────────────────────────────────── - Camera data: used only for live rep detection. Not stored. - Local session data: used to display your workout history and weekly rep totals inside the app. - Ad data: managed entirely by Google AdMob per their policies. ──────────────────────────────────────── 3. Third-Party Services ──────────────────────────────────────── FitStack uses the following third-party service: - Google AdMob (advertising) Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy Opt-out of personalized ads: https://www.google.com/settings/ads ──────────────────────────────────────── 4. Children's Privacy ──────────────────────────────────────── FitStack is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action. ──────────────────────────────────────── 5. Data Retention ──────────────────────────────────────── All workout data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time from the Settings screen inside the app. We have no access to this data. ──────────────────────────────────────── 6. Your Rights ──────────────────────────────────────── You can: - Clear all session history from Settings → Clear session history - Opt out of personalized ads via your device settings: Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID - Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data ──────────────────────────────────────── 7. Security ──────────────────────────────────────── All data processed by FitStack remains on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers by us. Ad-related data transmission is handled securely by Google. ──────────────────────────────────────── 8.
